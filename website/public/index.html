WHAT IS MATT?

Simple on purpose.

MATT is a community token built on Ronin. Its contract has a fixed supply, no taxes, no blacklist, no transfer limits, and no owner controls. Holders may burn their own tokens, and ERC-2612 Permit support provides modern approval compatibility.
